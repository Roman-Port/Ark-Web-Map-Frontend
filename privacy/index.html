Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy outlines how ArkWebMap uses and protects the information that you grant to it when you use the website or the app.

ArkWebMap will only use the information you provide to serve its purpose. Any data you enter will only be used in accordance with this privacy statement.

ArkWebMap may update this page from time to time. You should review this page periodically to check for updates and ensure you’re happy with any changes. You may request the closure of your account by writing rvporterfield@gmail.com.

What we collect

We collect the following information:

  • Your Steam ID, used for authentication
  • Your public Steam information, including your name and profile picture
  • Information you enter in supported games that are linked to the ArkWebMap companion program
  • The IP addresses you use to connect to the ArkWebMap service
  • Basic device information that cannot be linked back to you, such as device manufacturer and browser version

What we do with the information we collect

We require this information to make ArkWebMap operate, and for our own security and record-keeping.

  • We will never send promotional emails, or any emails unless otherwise requested by you or if an urgent notification is needed.
  • We will never pass your details to a third party for marketing or advertising.
  • In the event that ArkWebMap is sold or transferred to a third party, the collected information will be transferred to that third party and will fall under their privacy policy.
  • We will comply with reasonable law enforcement requests as needed.

Children’s privacy

ArkWebMap does not knowingly collect any information of any children under the age of 13, or allow such persons to register. ArkWebMap or its content are not directed at children under the age of 13. In the event that we learn that we have collected information of children under the age of 13 without parental consent, we will delete that information.

Security

We are invested in keeping your information safe and secure. To prevent unauthorized access, we have put in place suitable physical and electronic procedures to safeguard the information we collect from you.

Links to other websites

ArkWebMap may contain links (by ArkWebMap or by user submitted content) to other websites. However, once you leave ArkWebMap, we have no control over that other website. We are not responsible for the protection or privacy of any information you provide on other websites.

Controlling your personal information

You may manage your personal data by writing to rvporterfield@gmail.com. You may request your data or a closure of your account here.
